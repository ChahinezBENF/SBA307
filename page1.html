ICAO Certification

ICAO certification primarily involves Aerodrome Certification, which ensures that aerodromes meet international safety and operational standards.

The certification process includes training courses that cover the rationale for certification, procedures in accordance with ICAO requirements, and how certification enhances safety and efficiency in aviation operations.
